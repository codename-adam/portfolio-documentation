Our heritage began with a beautiful natural rock barn, built in 1918, which we have transformed into the Clubhouse - the center for many of our activities at Colorado Springs Country Club. Tastefully appointed, the Clubhouse ambience is comfortable and inviting, providing just the right place for entertaining friends and family with good food, parties and other social activities. The Clubhouse is also a perfect site for corporate events.

Our Lounge provides a warm and friendly pub-like atmosphere to watch a game with friends, unwind from a hectic week, or join in one of the many social activities.
